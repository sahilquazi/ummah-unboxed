How this field actually works

The text you write here is processed by a large language model (LLM) that reads your description and generates a preference profile. It looks for signals like regions you mentioned, types of objects you described, the feeling or aesthetic you're drawn to, and whether your language suggests you want something personal versus functional. That profile is then matched against our artisan database — maker by maker — to rank who fits you best. The more specific and honest your description, the more accurate the match. Vague answers like "I like handmade things" produce a much weaker result than "I want something connected to Palestinian women's craft traditions that I can use in my kitchen." There is no wrong answer, but specificity is rewarded.
